About StockVision

StockVision is a modern financial analytics dashboard designed to help users visualize, analyze, and understand stock market trends through interactive charts and structured market insights.

Data Visualization

Interactive charts for price trends, trading volume, price distribution, and percentage changes to simplify complex market data.

Market Insights

Market overview including major indices, volatility indicators, and top gainers & losers for quick decision-making.

Performance Focused

Lightweight and responsive design built for smooth performance across desktop and mobile devices.

Educational Purpose

Designed for learning, practice, and demonstration of financial data visualization concepts.
